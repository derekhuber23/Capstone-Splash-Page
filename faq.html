50% Business, 50% Computer Science About Our Program

The Integrated Product Development capstone project for CSB students spans two semesters. In the first semester, students spend their time working on the business end, developing a business plan and preparing both oral and written presentations before moving on the second semester. The second semester is where students start to implement their projects. With direction from their project sponsor, students develop and code the designs they constructed in the first semester.
